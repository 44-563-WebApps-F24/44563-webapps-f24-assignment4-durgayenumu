Venkata Durga Yenumula presents a Greek play by Sophocles

King Oedipus by Sophocles

Oedipus: What is this secret? Let me know at once!

Jocasta: Please, Oedipus, no! In heaven's name, don't ask!

Oedipus: I must know, no matter how dark the truth may be.

Jocasta: Then, I will speak. You were born to Laius.

Oedipus: My father? But... what of the prophecy?

Jocasta: It is true. The prophecy said you would kill him and marry your mother.

Oedipus: Oh, gods! My curse is fulfilled!

_Jocasta rushes out, grief-stricken._
