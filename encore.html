Venkata Durga Yenumula presents a Greek play by Sophocles

King Oedipus by Sophocles

Oedipus:

Tell me now, Jocasta, was I truly abandoned as a child?

Jocasta:

Yes, my love, it was done to prevent the prophecy from coming true.

Oedipus:

But fate cannot be outrun. The gods have brought me back to this cursed place!
